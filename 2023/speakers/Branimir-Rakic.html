AI-powered search with OriginTrail Decentralized Knowledge Graph

As we progress further into the AI era, how can we safeguard the integrity and trustworthiness of shared knowledge? Discover the new capabilities in OriginTrail Decentralized Knowledge Graph and learn how its core primitive, AI-ready knowledge assets enable knowledge sharing and trust in the age of AI. Designed to capitalize on network effects, OriginTrail enables a decentralized, AI-enhanced framework for advanced semantic search and querying applications, supported by a vibrant open-source community. OriginTrail's innovative fusion of knowledge graphs, blockchains, and AI technologies has already gained traction in various industries, with major US retailers such as Walmart, Target, and Home Depot, the British Standards Institution, and the Swiss Railway company among the organizations benefiting from cross-organizational knowledge sharing. Join Branimir's talk as he delves into the latest progress, developments, and challenges in the fascinating field of Decentralized Knowledge Graphs and AI. Get ready to expand your understanding of data management and collaboration in today's interconnected world.

Branimir Rakic

Founder & CTO @ Trace Labs (OriginTrail Core developers)
